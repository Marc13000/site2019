A Warp and Weft of Dance shows a feeling of oneness when the audience witnessed a cultural blend of three Indian classical dance forms together with the Western dance forms.
This performance was an amalgamation of Bharatnatyam, Kathak, Odissi and Contemporary, showcasing the unity in the diverse yet closely knit cultures of India.
The Contemporary Classics are Jamnabai Narsee School's in-house performance group.The team consists of our very own students of Jamnabai. With various forms of dance ranging from kathak to contemporary, these handful students symbolize talent, determination and confidence.
