The Nartana Flyers is Jamnabai Narsee School's high school based dance group. It fuses the foreign dance style with the Indian folk dance styles. Aerial is a foreign dance style in which the routine takes place on a silk cloth including various acrobatic poses. The Indian folk includes Hoja Giri from Tripura in which girls balance bottles on their heads and perform various stunts. Tera taali, a folk performed by locals in Rajasthan by beating manjeeras to create musical beats. Last but not the least Haryanvi a folk performed in Punjab where women wear beautiful long ghaghras while performing a graceful dance.
