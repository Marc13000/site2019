We're ecstatic to announce that TEDxYouth@JNS is growing, and is now in its second edition! Created in the spirit of TED's mission, 'Ideas Worth Spreading,' and our school motto, 'Knowledge is Strength Supreme', TEDxYouth@JNS exemplifies the conglomeration of intellect-stimulating and groundbreaking ideas. TEDxYouth@JNS will be held on the 5th of July, 2019, at Jamnabai Narsee School. So get ready to immerse yourself into a melting pot of a plethora of ideas and insights that will leave you inspired to challenge conventional ideas and make a difference!
